üéÆ Core Gameplay (What the Player Does)

In Lights Out, you play as Adam, a stressed office worker who wakes up to find his camping trip has become a nightmare‚Äîthe world is locked in eternal darkness. Your only tool for survival is your campfire and your wits. Explore the darkness, gather resources, and craft increasingly powerful light sources to push back the shadows. Manage your sanity meter carefully; spend too long in the dark and you'll lose your mind. Build structures for comfort and safety, expand your illuminated territory, and uncover what's really happening in this cursed place.

  • Exploration & Resource Gathering: Venture into the darkness to collect wood, stone, coal, fiber, and metal from trees, rocks, and ore deposits
  • Sanity Management: Stay near light sources and buildings to maintain sanity; darkness drains it rapidly
  • Crafting System: Combine resources to create torches, campfires, electric lights, and buildings with unique properties
  • Strategic Light Placement: Position light sources to maximize coverage and create safe zones while lighting up new areas
  • Base Building: Construct buildings that recover sanity and serve as safe havens from the darkness
  • Stamina System: Sprint across the map to reach distant resources, but manage your stamina carefully
  • Environmental Obstacles: Navigate through hazards like thorny plants that transform and disappear as light touches them
  • Progress Tracking: Watch the progress bar fill as you illuminate more of the map, driving toward victory
